Code of Conduct

Our mission is to reduce crime in neighborhoods. By sharing videos of suspicious or criminal activity with our neighbors, we can all work together to make our communities safer for all.

With Ring Neighborhoods, we want to foster an inclusive online community where all our neighbors feel welcomed and respected, and where they can share important and sometimes sensitive information with each other. To ensure that Ring Neighborhoods remains a platform for neighbors to respectfully interact with each other, we’ve created the following Community Guidelines.

Ring Neighborhoods depends on neighbors like you to ensure that our community treats each other with respect and civility. If you identify anything that violates our Community Guidelines, please flag the post and bring it to our attention. Keep in mind, however, that our Neighborhoods are filled with people with diverse beliefs, so just because you disagree with a neighbor, it doesn’t mean they violated our Community Guidelines.

Security-Related Videos Only

Ring Neighborhoods is a place to share and discuss videos of suspicious or criminal activity in your community. Videos that aren’t related to home security and those that contain inappropriate content will be removed immediately.

Treat Neighbors With Respect

While the occasional disagreement may occur, any attempts to attack, insult, shame, bully or belittle your neighbors will not be tolerated.

Don’t Discriminate

Our Neighborhoods are diverse and inclusive spaces for neighbors to safely interact with each other. Any posts or comments that are deemed discriminatory or prejudice to any group of people will be removed immediately.

Keep It Clean

Please make sure all posts are family friendly. Refrain from using vulgar or obscene language, and from discussing or joking about illegal or sensitive activities.

No Politics Allowed

Ring Neighborhoods is not a place to discuss politics. Any political endorsements, campaigning or ranting will not be tolerated.
